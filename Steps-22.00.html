With the Visualizations and Data panes open, click on the Export Destination map. Scroll down to Tooltips in the Visualizations pane. In the Data pane, expand the Measures table and drag State Export Concentration to Tooltips. Place it between EC and EE MoM%. Right click on State Export Concentration and click on Rename for this visual. Rename to State EC.

 

Now when you click on a State in the State map, State EC will also appear in tooltips for the Export Destination map. You cant apply filters to both maps at the same time but now once youve selected a state you can hover over any region or country and State EC will show you how much of the selected states export earnings that region or country accounts for.
