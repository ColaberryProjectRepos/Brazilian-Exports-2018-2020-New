Create Calendar table and relationship to Fact table 10


1. Navigate to the top left side of the screen and click on File. Click on Options and Settings, then click on Options.

2. A window will open, click on the Data Load tab under Current File. Uncheck the Auto date/time box and click on OK.


3. Click on the Data View tab and in the Table Tools tab select New Table

Type the following DAX formula into the DAX formula bar, 

Calendar = 

ADDCOLUMNS(
    CALENDAR(MINX(Dim_Date, Dim_Date[Date]), MAXX(Dim_Date, Dim_Date[Date])),
    "Year", YEAR([Date]),
    "Quarter", FORMAT([Date], "\QQ"),
    "Month", FORMAT([Date], "mmmm"),
    "Month Number", MONTH([Date]),
    "DateID", MONTH([Date]) & "0" & DAY([Date]) & YEAR([Date])
)

4. Replace Table = with it.

5. Right click on the newly created Calendar table in the Data tab and click on Mark as date table which will open a dropdown, click on Mark as date table again.

6. Click on Select column under Date Column, select Date, and click OK.

7. Click on the Model view tab on the left side of the screen.

8. Delete the relationship between Dim_Date and Fact_BrazilExports

9. Create a relationship between Calendar and Fact_BrazilExports by dragging and dropping DateID in Calendar on top of DateID in Fact_BrazilExports

10. Right click on Dim_Date in the Data pane and click on hide
