Add Line and clustered column chart 15


1. Click on Line and clustered column chart from the Build Visual tab of the Visualizations pane

  • Add Year and Month from Calendar, in that order, to the X-axis
  • Add Export Earnings Per Month to the Column y-axis, right click on Export Earnings Per Month and rename to EE Per Mth
  • Add Export Earnings MoM, Export Earnings YoY, and Export Concentration to the Line y-axis
  • Add Total Export Earnings, Export Concentration, Export Earnings MoM, and Export Earnings YoY to Tooltips. Right click each one and rename them to Total EE, EC, EE MoM%, and EE YoY%, respectively




2. Click on the Format visual tab and then the General tab. Expand the Properties tab and the Size tab, set Height to 327 and Width to 355, expand the Position tab and set Horizontal to 205 and Vertical to 71
 
3. Set the Title to ON and expand the Title tab, below Title Text type Month/Year, set Text color to #FBF12E and Background color to #44AA29. Set the Horizontal alignment to Center

4. Click on the Visual tab next to the General tab, expand the Y-axis tab, and set the Title tab to Off
 
5. Expand the Columns tab and set the color to #122e93
 
6. Expand the Lines tab, navigate to Colors and set EC to #B498F4, EE YoY% to #f1b183, and EE MoM% to #aae1c9

7. Hover over the Month/Year header and click on the up arrow to Drill up then click on the two down arrows to Go to the next level in the hierarchy
