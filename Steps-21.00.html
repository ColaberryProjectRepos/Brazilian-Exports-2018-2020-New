Postscript Change 21


Sometimes you may create a visual by suggestion of a higher up, stakeholder or end user. Only to find that it’s not such a good choice. 

 

Delete the middle Group/Industry Scatter chart. Expand the Data pane and expand Dim_Product. Click on the Sector/Industry/Product Line and clustered column chart and expand the Visualizations pane. Drag Industry Group to the X-axis prompt and place it between Sector and Industry. Expand Format your visual tab and click on the General tab. Expand Properties, expand Position and set Horizontal to 730 and Vertical to 71. Under Size set Height to 326 and Width to 549. Expand Title and rename to Sector/Group/Industry/Product.

 

Click on the Month/Year Line and clustered column chart. In the General Tab, under Properties set Height to 326 and Width to 529.
