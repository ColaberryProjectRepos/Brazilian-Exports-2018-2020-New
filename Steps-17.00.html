Add Line and clustered column chart 17


1. Click on Line and clustered column chart from the Build Visual tab of the Visualizations pane

  • Add Sector, Industry, and Product to X-axis
  • Add Export Earnings Per Month to Column y-axis, right click on it and rename it to EE Per Mth
  • Add Export Earnings MoM, Export Earnings YoY, and Export Concentration to Line y-axis. Right click on each one and rename them to EE MoM%, EE YoY%, and EC respectively
  • Add Total Export Earnings, Export Concentration, Export Earnings MoM, and Export Earnings YoY to Tooltips. Right click on Total Export Earnings and rename it to Total EE




2. Click on the Format visual tab, followed by the General tab. Expand Properties followed by Position, set Horizontal to 916 and Vertical to 71, expand Size, set Height to 326 and Width to 363
 
3. Expand Title, type Sector/Industry/Product, change Text color to #FBF12E, Background color to #44AA29, and set Horizontal Alignment to Center


4. Click on the Visual tab next to the General tab. Expand Y-axis and turn its subtab Title Off. Expand Columns and change color to #112e93. Expand the Lines tab, followed by the Colors tab. Set EC to #B498F4, EE YoY% to #f1b183, and EE MoM% to #aae1c9

5. Navigate to the top right corner of the Sector/Industry/Product chart and click on the ellipsis dots. Set the Sort axis to EE Per Mth and Sort descending
