Key Insights - Negative 5



  • Export earnings for the Materials sector (which is a key sector) in the state of Espirito Santo have decreased by 32% from 2018 to 2020. Espirito Santo’s export earnings, excluding the Materials Sector, have decreased by 10% and the materials sector accounted for 64.1% of the state’s export earnings from 2018-2020.


  • Export earnings for the Materials sector in Bahia have decreased by 15% from 2018-2020. The Materials sector accounts for 49.3% of Bahia’s export earnings, so a decrease of 15% in such a key sector is worrying. Export earnings for the state of Bahia have decreased by 1%, if you exclude the Materials sector export earnings have actually increased by 15%.
