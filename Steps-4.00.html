Key Insights - Positive 4



  • Export earnings in the state of Mato Grosso have gone up by 23.1% from 2018-2020. While Brazil has had a slight decrease in export earnings from 2018-2020, Mato Grosso has had a significant increase. The country as a whole has had a decrease in export earnings of 4.4% (6.06% excluding Mato Grosso)


  • In February 2020 Export earnings experienced their highest year over year increase. Export earnings for February 2020 increased by 72% when compared to February of 2019, while export earnings increased by 2.5% for February 2019 when compared to February of 2018.
