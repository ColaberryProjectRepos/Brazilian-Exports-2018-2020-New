Add Scatter chart 16



1. Click on Scatter chart from the Build Visual tab of the Visualizations pane.

  • Add Industry Group and Industry from Dim_Product to Values
  • Add Export Earnings Per Month to X-axis, right click on it and rename it to EE Per Mth
  • Add Export Earnings YoY to Y axis, right click on it and rename it to EE YoY
  • Add Total Export Earnings to Size, right click on it and rename it to Total EE
  • Add Total Export Earnings, Export Concentration, and Export Earnings MoM to Tooltips. Rename Export Concentration and Export Earnings MoM to EC and EE MoM% respectively




2. Click on the Format visual tab and then the General tab

  • Expand the Position tab and set Horizontal to 559 and Vertical to 71
  • Expand the Properties tab and the Size tab, set Height to 327 and Width to 356
  • Set the Title to ON and expand the Title tab, below Text type Group/Industry, set Text color to #FBF12E and Background color to #44AA29. Set the Horizontal alignment to Center




3. Click on the Visual tab next to the General tab, expand the X-axis tab, and then expand the Title tab and change Color to #112e93. Set the Y-axis On, expand the Y-axis tab, and then expand the Title tab and change Color to #F1B183

4. Expand the Markers tab, followed by the Color tab, and click on the fx Conditional formatting button
  • Select Gradient for Format style, select Export Earnings MoM for What field should we base this on?
  • For Minimum choose the color #CD4C46 and for Maximum choose the color #95DABB
  • Expand the Legend tab, followed by the Text tab, Bold the text and change the color to #AAE1C9

5. Expand the Category label tab, change the Font size to 8 and the Color to Black
